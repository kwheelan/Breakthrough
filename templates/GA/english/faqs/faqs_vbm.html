Q. How do I vote with my absentee ballot?

Follow the instructions that your county elections official provides with your ballot. You will receive all the forms and envelopes necessary for marking and return of the ballot. If you fail to complete your ballot correctly your ballot may not be counted.

If you registered by mail and did not submit a copy of one of the required forms of registration identification, you will receive notification with your ballot package. Copies of any IDs must be placed in the outer envelope. If you have any questions, please contact your county registrar.

Q. How do I return my absentee ballot? When is the deadline to return my absentee ballot?

Your absentee ballot must be received by your county registrar by 7 PM on Election Day (November 3, 2020).

You can either mail your absentee ballot via U.S. first class mail or hand deliver to your county registrar. If mailing, you must affix postage to the ballot envelope. Ballots received after 7 PM on Tuesday, November 3, 2020 will not be counted.
